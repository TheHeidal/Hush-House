> In the 18th century, a secretive cabal of scholars established the 'Curia of the Isle' to manage Hush House, making it a haven of knowledge until 1896 when a disgraced Librarian brought the House to penury.

After the [[Dewulfs|Dewulf]] line ended with [[Eva Dewulf]]'s (purported) death, in 1785[^7] a group of scholars established the Curia of the Isle, an organisation that controlled [[Hush House]] until it was bankrupt by [[Thirza Blake]][^9] and the [[Nocturnal Branch]] took control in 1896[^8].

Most members of the Curia were not [[Long]][^6], although [[Fraser Strathcoyne]] is immortal and the Ligeian [[Rowena]] likely held the position of Secretary Persistent.

The Curia had seven positions[^1]:
- [[#Librarians]]
- [[#Secretary Salvant]]
- [[#Secretary Vigilant]]
- [[#Secretary Enigmatic]]
- [[#Secretary Nunciant]]
- [[#Secretary Persistent]]
- [[#Treasurer]]

The Curia of the Isle has unclear connections to the [[House of Lethe]]. [[Ambrose Westcott]], the first librarian, was known to be a member[^10], and the three Vaults beneath were created during the Curia period.
# Librarians
The Librarian of Hush House is sheltered under [[the Watchman's Tree]], provided protection from Calyptra, and one of the only people allowed to use the [[encaustum terminale]][^15].

The librarian of Hush House were:

9. [[Brian Levinsen]]
10. [[Serena Blackwood]]
11. [[Gervinus van Lauren]]
12. [[The Twelfth Librarian]], player character of Book of Hours.
# Secretary Salvant
> The Secretary Salvant leads the Institute's healing and restoration efforts.

The only known secretary was [[Ehsan Fekri]]
# Secretary Vigilant
> The Secretary Vigilant ensures the Institute's safety.

The only known secretary was [[George Collers]][^4]
# Secretary Enigmatic
> The Secretary Enigmatic conceals or reveals the Institute's secrets.

The only known secretary was [[C.C.]]
# Secretary Nunciant
> The Secretary Nunciant communicates and negotiates with other organisations.

The only known secretary was [[Dr Ibn al-Adim]]
# Secretary Persistent
> The Secretary Persistent is ultimately responsible for the Institute's continuity.

The Secretary Persistent was likely [[Rowena]], who as a Ligeian is known to both be immortal and not eat in company[^3].
# Treasurer
> The Treasurer is responsible for the Institute's finances.

Known Treasurers:
- [[Fraser Strathcoyne]][^5]
